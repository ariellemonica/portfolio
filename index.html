About Me

My name is Monica Nadal. I live and work in Los Angeles, California. I have my bachelors from the University of California--Irvine in Film & Media Studies and Political Science. I am a media technology professional, specializing in finding creative technical solutions for clients, providing Software as a Service and Content as a Service.

As a consumer of different technologies and media, and with a background in marketing, media engineering, and as an avid admirer of art and design, I believe in interactions with our tools should not only be functional, but intuitive and aesthetically pleasing.

In my professional life, I live by a couple of mantras. The first is that the right answer is the right answer, no matter who it comes from. This means that no one should be afraid to contribute, and no one's ego should be too big to listen.

The second mantra I live by is that being a leader isn't about always being the smartest person in the room. In fact, I might go as far as to say that if you're all around the smartest person in the room, you're in the wrong damn room. Being a leader is about surrounding yourself with smart, capable people and supporting the people around you so you can all grow together.

In terms of professional interests, I am fascinated by web design, solutions architecture, media engineering, UI/UX, and the legal implications of intellectual property and privacy. My personal interests and hobbies include writing, photography, reading (fiction, non-fiction), hiking, and being quietly inappropriate in art museums. I do most of these things in the company of my dogs dog, Butler. (Rest in peace, Charlie).
